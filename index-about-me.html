About Me

I'm Kaliel Selhorst, 19 years old, a software developer focused on back-end and information security, passionate about creating scalable, modern, and well-structured solutions. I have experience in Java, Python, JavaScript, and Ruby on Rails, working on projects ranging from management and automation systems to complete web applications.

I have a special interest in cybersecurity, blockchain, applied AI, and RESTful API development, always seeking to integrate best security practices (LGPD, encryption, 2FA) and performance into projects.

Besides the technical side, I enjoy working with GitHub and team collaboration practices, using branches, versioning, and clear documentation to keep projects organized. I also explore creative areas, such as game development in HTML/CSS/JS and automations for data processing and smart reports.
